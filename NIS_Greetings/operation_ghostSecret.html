South Korea Cyber Laws and Regulations

1. Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA)

Purpose and Scope: Enacted in 2011, PIPA is South Korea’s comprehensive data protection law aimed at safeguarding personal user information and regulating how organizations handle personal data.

Key Provisions:

  • Organizations may be fined up to 3% of total revenue from the previous fiscal year.
  • Violators face up to 5 years in prison and additional fines up to 10 million KRW ($8,400 USD).
